I can't speak to the tslib issues with this, because it mostly just
worked for me, but I found it useful to build a test rig that let me
hit exactly the same spot on my LCD. Basically, a block of wood with a
few strips glued to it so I could register it against a corner of the
LCD, and a couple of holes drilled in it that I could slide a sharpened
dowel through.

This let me get fairly repeatable points on the touchscreen and helped
me figure out what was hardware.
