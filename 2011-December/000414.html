Thank you Lisandro.  I've found the problem.  As you've mentioned, the TSLIB_TSDEVICE environment variable is changing at every reboot/restart of the system.  Even though i use the exact device name, it still not work.  What i have to do is to check for the /dev/input/event that is associated with the eGalax TouchScreen and use that event# for the TSLIB_TSDEVICE and QWS_MOUSE_PROTO variables.
 
This mailing list is really awesome.  Thank you so much.
