Hi Phil.

> I have a touchscreen with an eGalax controller, it is used by the
> "usbtouchscreen" kernel module and appears as /dev/input/event1.
> 
> Should this device be supported with the "module_raw input" line in
> ts.conf?

I got that setup working modifying the plugin input-raw a bit. The
problem was that the eGalax with usbtouchscreen driver doesn't report
pressure, which the input driver seems to require.

As a very brute work-around solution (just to get it working) I just
added a hardcoded pressure in the EV_SYN handler:
samp->x = i->current_x;
samp->y = i->current_y;
samp->pressure = 10;

> If it isn't supported, would it be difficult to implement? I would
> really like to use tslib/kdrive in my embedded system, but I don't
> really have that much choice on the type of touchscreen controller.

I share your opinion. We use directfb in our embedded, and need to
support a variety of touchscreens, and tslib provides a nice
abstraction.
