Good.
Next step is to calibrate the touchscreen in order to transform range ts_min-ts_max
to device screen size. ts_calibrate is used for that. ts_print and ts_test rely on
calibrated device being present (existing /etc/pointercal, generated by ts_calibrate).

I suggest compiling tslib with --enable-debug=yes and/or running the offending util
under the gdb to see what is going on under the hood.

ts_calibrate 'should' work, since it only calculates the matrix values and uses
that same ts_read_raw() for input as ts_print_raw sees.
