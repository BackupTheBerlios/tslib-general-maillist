Opensuse...

Is this a USB device, and if so, have you tried creating a udev rule?  I've
been using something like:
"ACTION=="add", KERNEL=="event*", ATTRS{idVendor}=="xxxx",
ATTRS{idProduct}=="xxxx", MODE="666", SYMLINK+="yournamehere"

to create an alias, substituting your USB device's vendor and product ID,
and your symbolic name, obviously.  Then you can reference /dev/yournamehere
regardless of the event* to which it gets mapped.

http://reactivated.net/writing_udev_rules.html for more reading, but (at
least under Ubuntu 9.10) substitute "udevadm info" in place of "udevinfo" in
the examples.
