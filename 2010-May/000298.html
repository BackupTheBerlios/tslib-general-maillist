Hi,
we (me and two other guys) are trying to write a tslib plugin for the 
touchscreen of the palm pre.
But we are having some problems...

Our current code is hosted at github: 
http://github.com/playya/tslib/blob/master/plugins/cy8mrln-palmpre.c

Now to the touchscreen:
It's a capacitiv multitouch touchscreen that reports the events in a sort of 
strange way. The touchscreen is devided into 70 fields (7*10) and you get an 
intensity (pressure)  for every field. To get the position of the finger one has 
to interpolate the values of the different fields.

The device constantly reports data (in our configuration 60 pakets per second). 
We drop all pakets that contain no data.

Our current problem is that we can move around the courser, but we can't make 
a click event.
I expected that tslib will handle this but it seems we have to do something 
that it will recognize the click event.


So what do we have to do, to make tslib send a click event?

Thomas
