On 29.06.2011, 14:14 +0530 wrote conn intel:
> how can I configure tslib so as the QT understands the events coming
> from TSLIB?

QT-Embedded must build with tslib support.
See http://doc.qt.nokia.com/main-snapshot/qt-embedded-pointer.html

to define the pointer device :

#> export QWS_MOUSE_PROTO=tslib:/dev/input/touchscreen0
#> ./myQTApp -qws
